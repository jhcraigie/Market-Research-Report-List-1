<p><strong>The Inspection Chamber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotational Molding Inspection Chamber</li><li>Welded Piping Inspection Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inspection Chamber market comprises two main types: Rotational Molding Inspection Chambers and Welded Piping Inspection Chambers. Rotational Molding Inspection Chambers are made through a molding process where a heated resin is rotated to form the desired chamber shape. These chambers are known for their durability, resistance to corrosion, and ease of installation. On the other hand, Welded Piping Inspection Chambers are constructed through welding pipes and fittings together. These chambers are preferred for their high strength and rigidity, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
