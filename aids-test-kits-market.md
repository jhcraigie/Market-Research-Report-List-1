<p><strong>The AIDS Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AIDS Test Kits market consists of different types of tests for detecting the presence of HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus). Antibody tests are the most common, analyzing blood or saliva samples to detect HIV antibodies. RNA (viral load) tests measure the amount of HIV genetic material in the blood, often used to monitor the effectiveness of antiretroviral treatment. Antibody-antigen tests detect both HIV antibodies and antigens, enabling earlier detection of infection. These tests are vital in diagnosing HIV, determining the stage of infection, and assessing treatment options.</p></p>
