<p><p>There are three main types of skiing goggles in the market: cylindrical lens, spherical lens, and toric lens. Cylindrical lenses have a flat shape and provide good vertical vision, but may distort the view horizontally. Spherical lenses have a round shape and offer excellent optical clarity with minimal distortion, ensuring a wide field of view. Toric lenses combine the benefits of cylindrical and spherical lenses, correcting both vertical and horizontal distortions. Buyers can choose the lens type that best suits their skiing needs and preferences.</p></p>
