Main Distribution Boards (MDBs) are used to distribute electricity throughout a building, ensuring proper voltage levels and preventing circuit overloads. Emergency Distribution Boards (EDBs) are designed specifically for emergency situations, such as power outages or electrical failures. EDBs provide backup power to critical equipment or areas, safeguarding against potential disruptions.
