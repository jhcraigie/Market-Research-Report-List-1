<p><p>Water-based anti-corrosion coatings can be categorized into two main types: single-component coatings and multi-component coatings. Single-component coatings are ready to use and do not require any additional components for application. On the other hand, multi-component coatings require the blending of two or more components before application. Single-component coatings are simpler to use but may not offer as high performance as multi-component coatings, which typically provide better protection against corrosion due to their advanced formulations.</p></p>
