<p><ul><li>Nucleic Acid Test Kit</li><li>Antibody Test Kit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COVID-19 Test Kits can be categorized into two main types: Nucleic Acid Test Kits and Antibody Test Kits. Nucleic Acid Test Kits detect the genetic material of the virus using real-time polymerase chain reaction (PCR) technology. They are highly accurate but require complex laboratory facilities and skilled personnel. Antibody Test Kits, on the other hand, detect the presence of antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus. These kits provide quick results but are less accurate than Nucleic Acid Test Kits. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
