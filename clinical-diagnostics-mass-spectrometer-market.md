<p><p>The Clinical Diagnostics Mass Spectrometer Market is categorized into different types, including LC-MS, GC-MS, and Others. LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) combines the separation power of liquid chromatography with the sensitivity and selectivity of mass spectrometry. GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) utilizes gas chromatography to separate and analyze compounds before detection with a mass spectrometer. The "Others" category encompasses alternative mass spectrometry techniques used in clinical diagnostics. These different types of mass spectrometers enable accurate identification and quantification of various compounds in clinical samples, aiding in disease diagnosis and monitoring.</p></p>
