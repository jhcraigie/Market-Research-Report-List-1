<p><p>The 5G MVNO market consists of three types: Full MVNO, Light MVNO, and Thick MVNO. Full MVNOs have complete control over core network elements and infrastructure, allowing them to provide end-to-end services independently. Light MVNOs rely on wholesale partnerships to offer wireless services, reducing the need for extensive infrastructure investment. Thick MVNOs possess limited control over core network elements but can still offer a wide range of services by leveraging existing infrastructure. These market types highlight the varying degrees of control and infrastructure requirements that MVNOs have in the 5G market.</p></p>
