<p><p>Automatic microplate handlers are robotic systems designed to automate the process of handling and manipulating microplates used in laboratories. They can perform tasks like plate stacking, lid handling, and barcode scanning, increasing productivity and reducing human error. Semi-automatic microplate handlers, on the other hand, require some level of human intervention to operate. They still offer improved efficiency by automating certain plate handling tasks but rely on manual input for other tasks. Both types of handlers are widely used in research, pharmaceutical, and clinical laboratories for various applications.</p></p>
