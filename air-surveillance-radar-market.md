<p><p>The Air Surveillance Radar market includes two main types: Airborne Radars and Ground-Based Radars. Airborne Radars are radar systems installed on aircraft to detect and track other aircraft, missiles, and objects in the airspace. They provide situational awareness to pilots and are essential for air combat and navigation. Ground-Based Radars, on the other hand, are stationary radar systems positioned on the ground. They deliver long-range coverage of the airspace, detecting and identifying aircraft and other objects to ensure air traffic control and national security.</p></p>
