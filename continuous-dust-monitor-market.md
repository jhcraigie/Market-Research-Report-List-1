<p><p>A Continuous Dust Monitor (CDM) is a device used to measure and monitor the concentration levels of dust particles in the air continuously. Therefore, the demand for CDMs is increasing in various industries to improve air quality and safeguard employee health.</p><p>The Continuous Dust Monitor market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors.
