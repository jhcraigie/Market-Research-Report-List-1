<p><p>The AC-DC-EC Fans market encompasses various types of fans that are used for different purposes. Axial fans are designed for high airflow but low pressure applications. Tangential fans create a linear airflow and are commonly used in heating or cooling systems. Lastly, the "Others" category refers to fans that do not fit into the aforementioned types, such as crossflow fans or mixed flow fans.</p></p>
