<p><strong>The Torque Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Rheometer</li><li>Twin Screw Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Torque Rheometer market includes two main types: Single Screw Rheometer and Twin Screw Rheometer. A Single Screw Rheometer is a device used to measure the rheological properties of a material using a single rotating screw. It is commonly used to determine the viscosity and flow behavior of liquids and melts. On the other hand, a Twin Screw Rheometer has two screws that rotate in opposite directions, offering better mixing capabilities and enabling the measurement of more complex materials such as powders and solid-filled systems. Both types find extensive applications in industries requiring precise viscosity measurements and material characterization.</p></p>
