<p><p>Torsion test machines are used to determine the strength and mechanical properties of materials in torsional loading conditions. In the market, there are three types of torsion test machines - automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. </p><p>Automatic torsion test machines are fully automated, allowing for easy and quick testing without much human intervention. Semi-automatic torsion test machines require some human intervention during the testing process, such as loading and unloading the sample. Manual torsion test machines, on the other hand, rely on manual operation for each step of the testing procedure. The choice among these types depends on the level of automation and human involvement desired by the user.</p></p>
