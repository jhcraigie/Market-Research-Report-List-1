<p><p>The Inductive Linear Displacement Sensors market consists of three main types: Variable Reluctance Sensor, Differential Transformer Sensor, and Eddy Current Sensor. A Variable Reluctance Sensor measures distance by detecting changes in reluctance between a magnetic core and a coil. A Differential Transformer Sensor relies on the principle of magnetic induction to determine displacement accurately. An Eddy Current Sensor uses the principle of eddy currents induced in a conductive target to measure displacement. These sensors find applications in various industries where precise linear displacement measurement is required.</p></p>
