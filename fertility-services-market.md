<p><p>Fertility services refer to the various medical procedures and treatments that are designed to assist individuals or couples who are struggling with infertility and are seeking to conceive a child. Additionally, the advancements in reproductive technology and techniques have significantly improved success rates, thereby increasing the demand for fertility services.</p><p>Moreover, changing societal attitudes towards family planning and increased awareness about available fertility treatments are also contributing to the market growth. Furthermore, the expanding acceptance of same-sex marriages and partnerships has further fueled the demand for assisted reproductive technologies, such as sperm and egg donation and surrogacy, to achieve parenthood.</p><p>Technological advancements, such as preimplantation genetic testing (PGT) and three-parent IVF, are also driving the market forward.
