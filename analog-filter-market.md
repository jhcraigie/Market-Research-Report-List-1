<p><strong>The Analog Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pass Analog Filters</li><li>High Pass Analog Filters</li><li>Band Pass Filters</li><li>Notch Filters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog filter market consists of various types such as Low Pass, High Pass, Band Pass, Notch, and Others. Low Pass filters allow low-frequency signals to pass and attenuate higher frequencies. High Pass filters pass high-frequency signals while attenuating lower frequencies. Band Pass filters only permit signals within a specific frequency range to pass, blocking both low and high frequencies. Notch filters attenuate a specific frequency or range, typically removing unwanted signals. Other filters include All Pass, Band Reject, and Phase Shift filters, each designed for specific applications.</p></p>
