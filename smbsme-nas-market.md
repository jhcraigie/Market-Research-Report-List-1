<p><p>The SMB/SME NAS market comprises two primary types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise NAS solutions involve storing and managing data within an organization's physical infrastructure. This grants greater control and security but requires dedicated hardware and maintenance. Conversely, Cloud-based NAS operates through remote servers, enabling businesses to store and access data over the internet. This offers scalability, flexibility, and reduced hardware costs. Each market type caters to different needs, with on-premise providing localized control, while the cloud allows easy access from any location, making it suitable for businesses with distributed workforces.</p></p>
