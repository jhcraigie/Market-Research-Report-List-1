<p><p>The cardiac ultrasound transducers market includes different types of transducers used for cardiac imaging. These include sector transducers, microconvex transducers, linear transducers, pencil transducers, concave transducers, and others. Sector transducers are used for imaging large areas of the heart, while microconvex transducers are used for imaging small areas. Linear transducers produce high-resolution images of the heart's structures. Pencil transducers are used for detailed images of specific areas. Concave transducers provide a wider field of view. Other types include transducers with different specifications and sizes to suit specific imaging needs.</p></p>
