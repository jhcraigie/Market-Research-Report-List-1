<p><p>The Transformer Monitoring System market includes various types of monitoring systems used in transformers. Bushing Monitoring systems monitor the condition of the bushings in a transformer. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) devices are used to analyze the gases dissolved in transformer oil to identify potential faults. Partial Discharge (PD) monitoring systems detect and analyze the partial discharge occurring within the transformers. Other types of monitoring systems include temperature monitoring, oil level monitoring, and load monitoring, among others. These systems help ensure the efficient and safe operation of transformers by detecting any abnormalities or faults.</p></p>
