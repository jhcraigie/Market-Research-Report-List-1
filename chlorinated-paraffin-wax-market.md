<p><strong>The Chlorinated Paraffin Wax Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-grade</li><li>M-grade</li><li>H-grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chlorinated Paraffin Wax is classified into three different grades based on chlorine content - L-grade, M-grade, and H-grade. L-grade has the lowest chlorine content, typically ranging from 40-50%, making it suitable for applications where lower chlorine levels are required. M-grade has a higher chlorine content, usually between 50-60%, offering improved flame retardant properties compared to L-grade. H-grade has the highest chlorine content, exceeding 70%, making it ideal for applications where maximum flame retardancy is needed. Each grade caters to different industry needs and requirements.</p></p>
