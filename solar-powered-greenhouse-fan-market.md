<p><p>The solar-powered greenhouse fan market offers two main types: circular and exhaust fans. They help plants grow uniformly and reduce the risk of mold and disease. On the other hand, exhaust fans are designed to remove excess hot air and moisture from the greenhouse. By improving air circulation, these fans prevent overheating and create a healthier environment for plants to thrive.
