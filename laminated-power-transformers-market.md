<p><p>Laminated power transformers are categorized based on the number of phases they can handle. Single-phase transformers are designed for single-phase power systems, commonly used in residential and small commercial applications. Three-phase transformers are suitable for three-phase power systems, found in industrial and large commercial setups. Multiphase transformers can accommodate higher numbers of phases, such as six or nine, and are used in specialized power setups like railway systems or aerospace applications. These variations allow laminated power transformers to cater to different power distribution needs efficiently.</p></p>
