<p><p>Demand Response Management Systems (DRMS) are crucial tools for managing peak energy demand. There are two main types of DRMS markets: Conventional Demand Response and Automated Demand Response. </p><p>Conventional Demand Response relies on manual processes, where energy users respond to utility signals to reduce their consumption during peak periods. This approach requires active participation from consumers and can involve various methods like adjusting thermostats or turning off non-essential equipment.</p><p>In contrast, Automated Demand Response utilizes technology and communication systems to automatically adjust energy usage without human intervention. It relies on smart devices and sensors to enable real-time monitoring and automated responses, optimizing energy consumption and reducing peak demand.</p></p>
