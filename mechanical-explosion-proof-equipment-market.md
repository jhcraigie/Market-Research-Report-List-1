<p><p>Mechanical explosion-proof equipment refers to various types of equipment that are designed to prevent explosions or limit their impact in industrial settings. These include brakes, compressors, diffusers, gears, pneumatic actuators, axial fans, petrol filling equipment, pumps, solenoid valves, and others. Brakes are used to stop or slow down the motion of machinery, while compressors increase the pressure of gases or air. Diffusers help control the flow of fluid or gas, gears transmit power between rotating shafts, and pneumatic actuators convert energy into mechanical motion. Axial fans circulate air, petrol filling equipment ensures safe fueling procedures, pumps move fluids, solenoid valves control the flow of fluids or gases, and others refer to various explosion-proof equipment used in different industrial applications.</p></p>
