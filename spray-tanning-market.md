Spray tanning services are offered in various settings like salons, spas, and standalone tanning studios, providing convenience for customers. Additionally, the market is supported by the availability of a wide range of spray tanning products for at-home use, including sprays, lotions, and mousses.</p><p>In conclusion, the spray tanning market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. The increasing awareness of the harmful effects of traditional tanning methods and the desire for a sun-kissed appearance are among the key drivers for this growth.
