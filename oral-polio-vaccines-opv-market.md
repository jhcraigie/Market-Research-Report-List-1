<p><strong>The Oral Polio Vaccines (OPV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trivalent OPV</li><li>Bivalent OPV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral Polio Vaccines (OPV) market includes two types: Trivalent OPV and Bivalent OPV. Trivalent OPV targets all three strains of poliovirus, while Bivalent OPV targets only two strains. Trivalent OPV has been widely used in the past for polio eradication efforts, but due to concerns about vaccine-derived polio cases, many countries are transitioning to Bivalent OPV. Bivalent OPV provides protection against the two most prevalent strains of poliovirus, making it a more targeted and effective option for polio vaccination campaigns.</p></p>
