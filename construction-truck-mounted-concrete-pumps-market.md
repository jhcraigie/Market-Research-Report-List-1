<p><strong>The Construction Truck-Mounted Concrete Pumps Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Pumps</li><li>Boom Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction truck-mounted concrete pumps market mainly consists of two types of pumps: line pumps and boom pumps. Line pumps are used for horizontal pumping applications, where the concrete is transferred through pipes from the pump to the desired location. Boom pumps, on the other hand, are equipped with an articulating arm that can forcefully deliver concrete to higher and farther areas. Both types of pumps are extensively used in the construction industry to efficiently transport and distribute concrete for various projects.</p></p>
