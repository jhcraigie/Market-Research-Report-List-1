<p><p>Membrane filtration solutions are used to separate particles, molecules, and ions from a liquid or gas stream. There are different types of membrane filtration solutions available in the market - Microfiltration (MF), Ultrafiltration (UF), Nanofiltration (NF), and Reverse Osmosis (RO). Microfiltration uses a porous membrane to remove large particles, while Ultrafiltration removes smaller particles and macromolecules. Nanofiltration has a higher selectivity for divalent ions and organic molecules, while Reverse Osmosis is used to remove dissolved salts and impurities at a molecular level.</p></p>
