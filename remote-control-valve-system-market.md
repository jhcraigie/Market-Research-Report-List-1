<p><p>Remote control valve systems are categorized into three main types based on their operation mechanism - Hydraulic Valve Control System, Pneumatic Valve Control System, and Electric Valve Control System. </p><p>The Hydraulic Valve Control System uses hydraulic pressure to control the valve operation, the Pneumatic Valve Control System utilizes compressed air or gas, and the Electric Valve Control System relies on electric signals for valve control. Each type offers specific advantages and applications based on the requirements of the system.</p></p>
