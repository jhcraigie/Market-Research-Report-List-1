<p><p>The Catwalk System market consists of two main types: Flat Catwalk System and Rigged Catwalk System. In a Flat Catwalk System, a flat platform is suspended above the ground, allowing models to showcase garments while walking along its length. This system offers simplicity and ease of installation. On the other hand, a Rigged Catwalk System involves using rigging techniques to suspend the platform at varying heights, creating a dynamic and visually appealing runway. This system allows for versatile runway designs and a more dramatic fashion display. Both types serve as essential infrastructure in the fashion industry for organizing runway shows and enhancing the overall presentation.</p></p>
