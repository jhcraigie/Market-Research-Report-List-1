<p><p>Closed circuit water treatment chemicals market includes various types such as antifouling, corrosion inhibitors, antifreeze and deicing products, and biocides. Antifouling chemicals prevent the growth of organisms like algae, bacteria, and fungi in water systems. Corrosion inhibitors protect metal surfaces from rusting or corroding. Antifreeze and deicing products prevent the freezing or solidification of water in closed circuits. Biocides are chemicals that control or eliminate harmful microorganisms in water systems. These chemicals play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency, longevity, and cleanliness of closed circuit water systems.</p></p>
