<p><p>The antibody development services market includes two main types: monoclonal antibody development and polyclonal antibody development. Monoclonal antibody development refers to the production of antibodies that are identical, derived from a single cell line, and recognize a specific target. Polyclonal antibody development, on the other hand, involves the generation of antibodies from multiple cell lines, resulting in a mixture of antibodies that recognize various epitopes or targets. These services are crucial in pharmaceutical research and diagnostics, aiding in the understanding and treatment of diseases.</p></p>
