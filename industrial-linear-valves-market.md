<p><p>The industrial linear valves market comprises various types of valves including gate valves, diaphragm valves, and others. Gate valves are designed with a gate-like mechanism to control the flow of fluids by opening or closing the passage. Diaphragm valves use a flexible diaphragm to regulate the flow by pressing against a seat. Other types of linear valves include globe valves, pinch valves, and needle valves that are used in different industrial applications. These valves play a crucial role in ensuring efficient fluid flow management and are widely utilized in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
