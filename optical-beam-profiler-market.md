<p><strong>The Optical Beam Profiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Camera Based</li><li>Scanning Split</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The optical beam profiler market can be classified into two types: camera-based and scanning. Camera-based beam profilers capture the image of the laser beam on a sensor using a camera, providing a real-time visualization of the beam profile. Scanning beam profilers, on the other hand, use a motorized scanning mechanism to systematically sample across the laser beam, allowing for detailed measurements of its power and spatial distribution. These different types cater to varying requirements and applications in industries such as manufacturing, telecommunications, and healthcare.</p></p>
