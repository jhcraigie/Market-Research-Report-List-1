<p><p>The teeth whitening light market consists of three types: LED, UV, and Infrared. LED lights are commonly used in at-home teeth whitening kits and emit a blue light that reacts with the whitening gel to remove stains. UV lights, typically used in professional settings, are more powerful and accelerate the whitening process by breaking down the gel's ingredients. Infrared lights are mainly used for laser teeth whitening procedures and penetrate deep into the tooth to whiten from within. Each type of teeth whitening light offers different benefits and applications for achieving a brighter smile.</p></p>
