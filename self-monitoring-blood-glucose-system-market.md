<p><p>Self-monitoring blood glucose systems consist of two main components: blood glucose test strips and blood glucose meters. Blood glucose test strips are small, disposable strips that are inserted into the glucose meter to measure the glucose levels in a person's blood sample. The blood glucose meter, on the other hand, is a portable device that provides a digital readout of the glucose concentration in the blood. These two components work together to enable individuals with diabetes to monitor and manage their blood glucose levels at home.</p></p>
