<p><p>The indoor circuit breaker market can be categorized into three types: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage circuit breakers are commonly used in residential and small commercial buildings to protect electrical circuits from overload or faults. Medium voltage circuit breakers are suitable for larger commercial and industrial applications, providing protection against higher voltage levels. High voltage circuit breakers are designed specifically for utility substations and power plants to handle extremely high voltage levels and ensure grid reliability. These different types cater to varying voltage requirements of different sectors.</p></p>
