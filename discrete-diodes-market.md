<p><p>The discrete diodes market includes various types of diodes. Schottky diode is a semiconductor diode with a low forward voltage drop, used in high-frequency applications. Transient Suppression Diode (TVS) is employed to protect electronic devices from voltage spikes. Commutation diodes are utilized for switching circuits to control the current flow direction. Other diodes in the market encompass laser diodes, varactor diodes, Zener diodes, etc. These diodes find applications in areas like telecommunications, automotive, consumer electronics, industrial, and healthcare sectors, contributing to the growth of the discrete diodes market.</p></p>
