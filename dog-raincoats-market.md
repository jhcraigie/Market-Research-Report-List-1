<p><p>Dog raincoats come in various market types, including cape type, one-piece four-legged, and split four-legged designs. Cape-type raincoats are designed like a cape that covers the back and chest of the dog, leaving the legs free. One-piece four-legged raincoats cover the entire body of the dog, including all four legs, providing full protection from rain. Split four-legged raincoats also cover the entire body, but they have a divide between the front and back legs, allowing easier movement for the dog. Each market type offers different levels of coverage and mobility for dogs during rainy weather.</p></p>
