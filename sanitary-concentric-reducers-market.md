<p><p>The Sanitary Concentric Reducers market consists of two main types: Sanitary Welded Concentric Reducers and Sanitary Clamped Concentric Reducers. Sanitary Welded Concentric Reducers are fittings used in the plumbing industry to connect two pipes of different diameters in a smooth and hygienic way. They are permanently welded to ensure a secure and leak-free connection. On the other hand, Sanitary Clamped Concentric Reducers are designed for easy installation and removal, using a clamp to hold the reducer in place. These reducers offer flexibility and are commonly used in industries where frequent disassembly is required.</p></p>
