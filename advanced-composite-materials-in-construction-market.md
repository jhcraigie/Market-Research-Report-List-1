<p><p>Advanced composite materials are widely used in the construction industry due to their superior strength, lightweight nature, and corrosion-resistant properties. They can be classified into different market types based on their matrix material. Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC) consist of reinforcement fibers embedded in a polymer matrix, offering high strength and flexibility. Metal Matrix Composites (MMC) combine metal and ceramic fibers, providing enhanced thermal conductivity and strength. Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC) utilize ceramic fibers embedded in a ceramic matrix, offering high-temperature stability and excellent mechanical properties. Other market types include composites with unique matrix compositions, tailored for specific construction requirements.</p></p>
