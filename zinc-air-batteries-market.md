<p><strong>The Zinc-Air Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary (Non-rechargeable)</li><li>Secondary (Rechargeable)</li><li>Mechanical Recharge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zinc-air batteries come in two main types: Primary (non-rechargeable) and Secondary (rechargeable). Primary batteries are designed for one-time use and cannot be recharged, making them ideal for devices with low power requirements. Secondary batteries, on the other hand, can be recharged multiple times, providing a more sustainable and cost-effective option for larger electronics. Additionally, there is a growing market for mechanical recharge systems, where zinc-air batteries can be mechanically recharged through a process such as replacement of the zinc anode.</p></p>
