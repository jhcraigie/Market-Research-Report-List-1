<p><p>Bakery release agents are used to prevent products from sticking to baking surfaces, ensuring easy release. These agents come in three main market types: liquid, solid, and semi-solid. Liquid release agents are usually sprayed onto baking surfaces and provide a thin coating. Solid release agents are typically in the form of blocks or bars that are rubbed onto the surface or melted for application. Semi-solid release agents are in paste or gel form and spread easily, forming a thicker coating. Each market type offers various benefits, allowing bakery professionals to choose based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
