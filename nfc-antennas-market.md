<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>NFC antennas are categorized into two types: internal and external. Internal NFC antennas are embedded within electronic devices, such as smartphones or tablets, and are not visible from the outside. They are designed to provide efficient near field communication capabilities without compromising the appearance or design of the device. On the other hand, external NFC antennas are separate components that can be mounted or connected to devices externally. They are often used in applications where the internal antenna's performance may be hindered, such as in-wall readers or payment terminals.</p></p>
