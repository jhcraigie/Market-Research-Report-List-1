<p><p>Industrial food milling machines can be categorized into two types: vertical milling machines and horizontal milling machines. Vertical milling machines have a vertical spindle axis that allows for the cutting tool to move up and down, giving it flexibility and precision in the machining process. On the other hand, horizontal milling machines have a horizontal spindle axis, which makes them suitable for heavy-duty cutting operations. Both types of machines are widely used in the food industry to efficiently process and mill various types of food products.</p></p>
