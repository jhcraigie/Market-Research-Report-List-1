<p><p>Molded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCBs) are categorized into two types based on their trip unit mechanisms: electromechanical trip unit and electronic trip unit. The electromechanical trip unit uses mechanical components to detect and interrupt electrical faults, such as short circuits or overloads. On the other hand, the electronic trip unit employs microprocessors and sensors to monitor currents and voltage levels, offering advanced protection features and precise fault detection. These different trip unit types provide users with options to choose the most suitable circuit breaker for their specific needs and applications.</p></p>
