<p><p>Aerospace plastic flame retardant market types include antimony oxide, aluminum trihydrate, organophosphates/phosphorous, and boron compounds. Antimony oxide is commonly used for its high effectiveness in preventing flame spread, while aluminum trihydrate offers a cost-effective solution for reducing flammability. Organophosphates/phosphorous compounds provide excellent thermal stability and smoke suppression, while boron compounds are known for their environmentally friendly properties. These different types of flame retardants cater to various needs within the aerospace industry, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
