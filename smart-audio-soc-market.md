<p><p>The Smart Audio SoC market consists of two types based on the manufacturing process: 28nm process and 40nm process. The 28nm process involves the fabrication of transistors and other components at a scale of 28nm, resulting in improved performance and energy efficiency for audio SoCs. On the other hand, the 40nm process utilizes a larger scale, which may offer lower costs and simpler design. Both types cater to different market segments, with the 28nm process being preferred for high-performance and power-sensitive applications, while the 40nm process offers cost-effective solutions for less demanding audio SoC applications.</p></p>
