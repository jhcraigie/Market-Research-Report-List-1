<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) Kit Market includes two major types: Short-read Sequencing and Long-read Sequencing. Short-read sequencing involves high-throughput sequencing of small fragments, resulting in short reads. It is cost-effective and widely used for applications like genome assembly, variant calling, and transcriptome analysis. On the other hand, Long-read sequencing generates longer reads, enabling better analysis of complex genomic regions, structural variations, and gene isoforms. Although long-read sequencing is more expensive, it offers superior accuracy and allows researchers to investigate highly repetitive regions and large genomic rearrangements.</p></p>
