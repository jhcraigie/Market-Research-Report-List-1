<p><p>Coffee pods and capsules are available in two main types: paper type and metal type. Paper type coffee pods are made from biodegradable materials such as paper, tea bag filter material, or plant-based fibers. These pods are environmentally friendly and can be easily recycled or composted. On the other hand, metal type coffee pods are usually made from aluminum or stainless steel. These pods have a longer shelf life and can provide a stronger seal to preserve the freshness and flavor of the coffee. However, they are not biodegradable and may contribute to environmental waste if not properly disposed of.</p></p>
