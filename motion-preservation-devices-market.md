Dynamic stabilization devices provide flexibility and support to the spine, maintaining its natural motion. Artificial disc replacement devices replace damaged discs with artificial ones, preserving mobility. Annulus repair devices repair the damaged outer layer of the intervertebral disc to prevent disc herniation. Nucleus disc prosthesis devices replace the inner nucleus of the disc with an artificial prosthesis, preserving disc height and function. These devices offer alternatives to traditional spinal fusion surgeries, preserving motion and reducing pain.</p></p>
