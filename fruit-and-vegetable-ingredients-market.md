<p><p>The fruit and vegetable ingredients market includes various types of products. Concentrates are made by removing water from fruit or vegetable juice, resulting in a concentrated form with enhanced flavor. Pastes and purees are made by crushing or blending fruit or vegetable flesh, often used for manufacturing baby food or sauces. Pieces and powders involve cutting or drying fruits and vegetables into smaller pieces or powder form, used for baking or as flavoring. NFC (Not From Concentrate) juices are made by extracting juice without water removal, preserving the natural taste and nutrients.</p></p>
