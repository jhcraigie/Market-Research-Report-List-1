<p><p>The Grain & Seed Cleaning Equipment market is categorized into two types: Pre-Cleaning Type and Fine Cleaning Type. </p><p>The Pre-Cleaning Type equipment is designed to remove large impurities such as stones, sticks, and other debris from grains and seeds before the actual cleaning process. This type of equipment helps in enhancing the efficiency of fine cleaning machines by reducing the load of coarse impurities.</p><p>On the other hand, the Fine Cleaning Type equipment is specifically designed to eliminate smaller impurities like dust, chaff, and immature or broken grains. It ensures a higher level of purity and quality in the end product.</p></p>
