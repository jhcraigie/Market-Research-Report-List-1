<p><p>Home theater audio systems are designed to enhance the audio experience of watching movies or listening to music at home. There are two main types of home theater audio systems: front systems and surround systems. Front systems comprise speakers placed in the front of the room to create a more immersive audio experience. Surround systems consist of multiple speakers strategically located around the room, providing a 360-degree audio experience. Both types aim to recreate a cinematic sound experience and enhance the overall entertainment experience at home.</p></p>
