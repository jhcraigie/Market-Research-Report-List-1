<p><p>Bakery ovens can be categorized into different types based on their functionality and design. A convection oven is equipped with a fan that circulates hot air, ensuring even heat distribution and quick baking. On the other hand, a deck oven features a stationary baking surface, offering the traditional baking experience with radiant heat from the deck's surface. Both types are used in commercial bakeries but differ in their baking techniques. Convection ovens excel in baking various products simultaneously, while deck ovens are preferred for artisanal bread and pizza baking.</p></p>
