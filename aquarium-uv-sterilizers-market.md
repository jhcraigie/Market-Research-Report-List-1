<p><p>The aquarium UV sterilizers market includes various types such as tube type-dry bulb, tube type-wet bulb, and others. On the other hand, tube type-wet bulb sterilizers are equipped with a sleeve that allows water to flow over the bulb, maximizing UV exposure and enhancing sterilization.
