<p><p>A magnetoresistive element is a type of sensor that detects changes in the magnetic field by measuring the resistance of a material. It consists of thin films of ferromagnetic and non-magnetic materials sandwiched together. When a magnetic field is applied, the resistance of the sensor changes, allowing for the measurement of the field.</p><p>The magnetoresistive element market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for magnetic sensors in various applications, such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial sectors, is driving market growth. The automotive industry, in particular, is a major consumer of magnetoresistive elements due to their application in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), electric power steering systems, and gear position detection.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of Internet of Things (IoT) devices is further propelling the demand for magnetoresistive elements. These elements are used in IoT devices for various functions such as proximity sensing, position sensing, and current sensing. Magnetoresistive elements are extensively used in fitness trackers, smartwatches, and healthcare devices.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing various technological advancements and product innovations.
