<p><p>An electric skylight is a window installed on the roof of a building that can be opened and closed electronically, allowing natural light and fresh air to enter the interior space. Electric skylights are equipped with a motorized system that operates them using remote controls, wall switches, or smartphone apps. They are designed to provide convenience, energy efficiency, and enhanced comfort for homeowners and building occupants.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the electric skylight market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, there is a growing demand for energy-efficient solutions in residential and commercial buildings. Electric skylights contribute to energy savings by enabling natural lighting, which reduces the need for artificial lighting during the day. Additionally, electric skylights with smart control systems can be integrated with home automation systems, further enhancing their energy-saving capabilities.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on environmental sustainability and the need for improved indoor air quality are driving the adoption of electric skylights. These skylights not only provide natural light but also facilitate proper ventilation, allowing fresh air to circulate within the building. This is crucial for maintaining a healthy indoor environment, especially in areas with limited ventilation options.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology, such as the integration of sensors and automation features, are expected to drive the market growth. These technological enhancements provide better control, convenience, and safety to users. For instance, some electric skylights come with rain sensors that automatically close the skylight when it detects rain, preventing water leakage and damage to the interior.</p><p>In conclusion, the electric skylight market is projected to experience significant growth due to the increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions, environmental sustainability trends, and technological advancements. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric skylight market is divided into two main types based on the type of roofs they are installed on: pitched roof and flat roof. Pitched roof skylights are designed for sloping roofs, allowing natural light and ventilation into the space below. They often come in various shapes and sizes to accommodate different roof angles. On the other hand, flat roof skylights are designed for horizontal roofs and offer excellent thermal insulation while bringing in natural light. Both types cater to specific roof designs and provide benefits such as energy efficiency and improved indoor aesthetics.</p></p>
