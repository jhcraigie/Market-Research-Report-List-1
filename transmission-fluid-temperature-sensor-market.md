<p><p>The transmission fluid temperature sensor market consists of various types of sensors that measure the temperature of transmission fluid. These types include bimetallic temperature sensors, temperature sensor ICs, thermistors, resistive temperature detectors (RTDs), thermocouples, infrared temperature sensors, fiber optic temperature sensors, and others. Bimetallic sensors rely on the expansion of metals with temperature, while temperature sensor ICs use integrated circuits to measure temperature. Thermistors use the change in electrical resistance with temperature, RTDs use the variation in electrical resistance of metals, thermocouples use voltage changes, and infrared and fiber optic sensors utilize different methods for temperature measurement.</p></p>
