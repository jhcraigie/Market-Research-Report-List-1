<p><strong>The Turntable Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MM</li><li>MI</li><li>MC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turntable cartridge market is divided into three types: MM, MI, and MC. MM, or Moving Magnet, cartridges are the most common due to their affordability and compatibility with various turntables. MI, or Moving Iron, cartridges offer better sound quality by using a vibrating iron element. MC, or Moving Coil, cartridges are the most advanced and provide the best audio performance, but are also the most expensive. These cartridges work by moving a coil within a magnetic field, producing a highly detailed sound reproduction.</p></p>
