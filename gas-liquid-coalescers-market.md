<p><p>Gas-liquid coalescers are used to separate gas and liquid in various industrial processes. They come in two types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical gas-liquid coalescers are designed with vertical vessels to separate gas and liquid by gravity. They are ideal for low flow rates and low liquid loadings. Horizontal gas-liquid coalescers, on the other hand, are designed with horizontal vessels and are more suitable for high flow rates and high liquid loadings. Both types are used in industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, and wastewater treatment for efficient separation of gas and liquid.</p></p>
