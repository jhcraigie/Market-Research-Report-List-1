<p><p>The Covid-19 Rapid Diagnostic Test Market includes three major types: Immunoassay, Genomic and WGS, and rRT-PCR. Immunoassay tests detect antibodies produced by the body in response to the virus. Genomic and WGS tests analyze the genetic material of the virus to identify its strain. rRT-PCR tests detect the presence of viral RNA to confirm an active infection. Each test type has its own advantages and limitations, but collectively they play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring Covid-19 cases.</p></p>
