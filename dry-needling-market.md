<p><ul><li>J- Type</li><li>D-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry needling market can be classified into two types: J-Type and D-Type. J-Type needles have a characteristic curved shape resembling the letter "J" and are commonly used for dry needling techniques. They are ideal for targeting trigger points and specific muscle areas due to their design. D-Type needles, on the other hand, have a straight shape similar to the letter "D" and are mainly used for deep tissue penetration and broader muscle stimulation. These different market types cater to the specific needs and preferences of practitioners and patients in the field of dry needling.</p></p>
