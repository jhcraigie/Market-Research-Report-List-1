<p><p>Intraoral scanners are devices used for taking digital impressions of the teeth and oral cavity. There are three market types for these scanners: Cart, Pod, and Chair Integration. In the Cart market, the scanner is mounted on a mobile cart for flexibility and ease of movement. In the Pod market, the scanner is located in a fixed position and the patient is required to move to the scanner. In the Chair Integration market, the scanner is integrated into the dental chair, allowing for seamless and efficient scanning during procedures. These different market types cater to different needs and preferences of dental professionals.</p></p>
