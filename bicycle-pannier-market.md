<p><p>The bicycle pannier market can be divided into two main types: waterproof and non-waterproof. Waterproof panniers are designed to keep the contents dry even in wet conditions, typically made with materials such as PVC or nylon with waterproof coatings or liners. These panniers are ideal for cyclists who frequently ride in rainy areas or need to protect their belongings from water damage. On the other hand, non-waterproof panniers are made from materials like canvas or polyester without any water-resistant features. They are suitable for cyclists who mainly ride in dry weather conditions and prioritize affordability over waterproof functionality.</p></p>
