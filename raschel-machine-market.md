<p><p>A Raschel machine is a knitting machine used to produce various types of fabrics. It is named after its inventor, Julius Raschel, and is widely used in the textile industry for the production of netting and lace fabrics. The machine uses a series of latch needles to create intricate patterns and designs by interlocking the yarns. Raschel machines are known for their versatility and can produce a wide range of fabrics including rigid and stretchable textiles.</p><p>The global Raschel machine market has witnessed steady growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory during the forecast period.
