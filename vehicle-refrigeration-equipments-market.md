<p><p>Vehicle refrigeration equipment market can be categorized into two types: single temperature type and multi-temperature type. Single temperature type refers to refrigeration systems that maintain a consistent temperature throughout the vehicle, typically used for transporting one type of goods. On the other hand, multi-temperature type refers to refrigeration systems that have multiple compartments, each maintaining a different temperature. This type is suitable for transporting various goods with different temperature requirements, allowing for more flexibility and efficiency in the transportation of perishable goods.</p></p>
