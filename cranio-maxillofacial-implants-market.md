<p><p>Cranio Maxillofacial (CMF) implants refer to medical devices used in the treatment of facial defects or injuries. There are two main types of CMF implants: internal fixators and external fixators. Internal fixators are implanted inside the body and are used to stabilize fractured facial bones during the healing process. On the other hand, external fixators are applied externally and help support fractured facial bones from outside the body. Both types of implants play a crucial role in restoring the structure and function of the cranio maxillofacial region.</p></p>
