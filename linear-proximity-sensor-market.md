<p><p>Linear proximity sensors are used to detect the position or presence of an object in its proximity. There are different types of linear proximity sensors available in the market. One type is the resistive linear proximity sensor, which functions by measuring the change in resistance caused by the presence of an object. Another type is the magnetoresistive linear proximity sensor, which utilizes the change in magnetic field caused by an object to detect its presence. Both types of sensors are widely used in various applications requiring accurate object detection.</p></p>
