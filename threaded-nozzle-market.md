<p><strong>The Threaded Nozzle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread Nozzle</li><li>External Thread Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threaded nozzle market types include internal thread nozzles and external thread nozzles. Internal thread nozzles have threads on the inside, allowing them to be connected to a threaded outlet such as a pipe or hose. These nozzles are commonly used in industrial applications where a secure connection is needed. On the other hand, external thread nozzles have threads on the outside, enabling them to be connected to a threaded inlet such as a nozzle body. These types of nozzles are often utilized in consumer applications like garden hoses and spray bottles.</p></p>
