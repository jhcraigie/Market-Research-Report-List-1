<p><p>Nanometer titania is available in two main types: Rutile and Anatase. Rutile titania is known for its high refractive index and superior UV absorption properties, making it ideal for use in sunscreen and cosmetics. Anatase titania, on the other hand, is valued for its high photocatalytic activity, making it ideal for applications in environmental remediation and self-cleaning surfaces. Both types of nanometer titania have unique properties that make them suitable for different markets and applications.</p></p>
