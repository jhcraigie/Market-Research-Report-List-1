<p><p>Static Transfer Systems (STS) are devices used in electrical power systems to facilitate uninterrupted power supply to critical loads during power disturbances or failures. STS enable seamless transfer of load from the primary power source to a backup power source, such as a generator or an uninterruptible power supply (UPS), without any interruption or disturbance to the connected loads. These systems are highly reliable, efficient, and offer quick transfer times, ensuring that critical systems remain unharmed.</p><p>The future outlook of the STS market is promising, with a significant growth potential.
