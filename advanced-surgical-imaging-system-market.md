<p><strong>The Advanced Surgical Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image intensifier C-arms</li><li>Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advanced Surgical Imaging System refers to the use of specialized imaging devices during surgical procedures. Image intensifier C-arms are a type of system that use an intensifier tube to convert X-ray images into visible light, providing real-time fluoroscopic visualization. On the other hand, Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms) utilize a flat panel detector to directly capture X-ray images, improving image quality and reducing radiation exposure. Both systems are commonly used in various surgical procedures to aid surgeons in obtaining clear and detailed images, leading to better patient outcomes.</p></p>
