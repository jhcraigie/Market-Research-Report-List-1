<p><p>Clinical trial data management software is available in two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and operated on the organization's own servers and infrastructure. It provides complete control over data management but requires IT resources for installation and maintenance. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on a remote server and accessed over the internet. It offers increased accessibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for infrastructure maintenance. However, it relies on stable internet connectivity for optimal performance.</p></p>
