<p><strong>The Earth Leakage Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1P</li><li>2P</li><li>3P</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Earth leakage protection is a crucial feature in electrical systems to safeguard against electric shocks and fire hazards caused by leakage currents. The market for such protection can be classified into different types based on the number of poles (P) involved. 1P refers to single-pole protection, typically used in residential settings. 2P denotes double-pole protection, commonly found in commercial and industrial environments. 3P signifies three-pole protection, mainly used in three-phase systems. The "Other" category encompasses specialized types such as residual current circuit breakers with overcurrent protection and ground-fault circuit interrupters. These various types cater to different requirements and electrical configurations to ensure optimal safety.</p></p>
