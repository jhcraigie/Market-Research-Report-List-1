<p><p>A Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter is a medical device used in peritoneal dialysis, a type of kidney dialysis that removes waste products and excess fluid from the body by using the peritoneum, a membrane lining the abdominal cavity, as a natural filter. The catheter is inserted surgically into the abdominal cavity and serves as a conduit for the flow of dialysis fluid into and out of the peritoneal cavity.</p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the rising prevalence of chronic kidney diseases, such as end-stage renal disease (ESRD), is increasing the demand for dialysis treatment options, including peritoneal dialysis. Additionally, the growing geriatric population and the increasing prevalence of diabetes and hypertension, leading causes of kidney diseases, are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in catheter design and improved surgical techniques are driving the adoption of peritoneal dialysis catheters. Newer catheter designs aim to reduce complications, such as infection and catheter dislodgement, thereby improving patient outcomes. Additionally, the development of antimicrobial coatings for catheters has further enhanced their usage.</p><p>Furthermore, the shift towards home-based dialysis treatments and the advantages offered by peritoneal dialysis, such as improved quality of life, cost-effectiveness, and flexibility, are also contributing to the growth of the market.</p><p>Overall, the Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.60% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing prevalence of kidney diseases, advancements in catheter technology, and the growing preference for home-based dialysis treatments.</p></p>
