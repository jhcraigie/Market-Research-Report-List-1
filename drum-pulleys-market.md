<p><p>Drum pulleys are an essential component in various conveyor systems. The market for drum pulleys can be categorized into two types: support pulleys and drive pulleys. Support pulleys are designed to bear the weight of the conveyor belt and provide stability to the system. They are commonly found at the tail end of the conveyor. On the other hand, drive pulleys transmit power to move the conveyor belt, typically located at the head end. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring efficient and reliable operation of conveyor systems.</p></p>
