<p><p>The medical device analytical testing outsourcing market encompasses various types of testing services. Extractable & leachable testing involves evaluating potential substances that can be extracted from the device and could potentially pose a risk to patients. Material characterization focuses on identifying and analyzing the composition and properties of the device's materials. Physical testing examines the mechanical and physical properties of the device. Bioburden testing determines the level of microbial contamination on the device. Sterility testing ensures that the device is free from viable microorganisms. Lastly, other testing services may include corrosion testing, packaging testing, and validation testing.</p></p>
