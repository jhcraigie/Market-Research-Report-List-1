<p><p>Lift Destination Control System (DCS) is an advanced technology used in elevators that aims to optimize the movement of people by assigning passengers to specific elevators based on their desired destination floors. It eliminates the need for traditional floor buttons inside the elevator and instead requires passengers to input their desired floor on a centralized control panel located at the elevator lobby. The DCS then assigns the most efficient elevator to serve each passenger's desired floor, reducing waiting times and improving overall efficiency.</p><p>The global Lift Destination Control System market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
