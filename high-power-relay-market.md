<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the high power relay market, there are two main types: closed type and open type. Closed type relays are enclosed in a protective casing, providing insulation and shielding against electromagnetic interference. They are commonly used in harsh environments where there is a need for increased durability and safety. On the other hand, open type relays do not have a protective casing and are more compact. These relays are suitable for applications where space is limited and cooling is efficient.</p></p>
