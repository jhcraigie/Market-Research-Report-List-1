<p><p>In the premise cable market, there are two main types of cables: copper cable and fiber optic cable. Copper cables are made of copper wires and are widely used for transmitting electrical signals. They are less expensive compared to fiber optic cables but have limitations in terms of bandwidth and distance. On the other hand, fiber optic cables consist of thin strands of glass or plastic that transmit data as pulses of light. They offer high bandwidth, faster transmission speeds, and longer distance capabilities, making them suitable for advanced communication networks.</p></p>
