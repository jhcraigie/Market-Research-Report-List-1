<p><p>Lip brushes can be categorized into two market types based on their bristle material: animal hair and artificial hair. Animal hair brushes are made from natural fibers obtained from animals like squirrels, goats, or sables. They offer high quality and durability, ensuring smooth and precise application of lip products. On the other hand, artificial hair brushes are crafted from synthetic materials, offering a cruelty-free alternative. They provide a similar level of performance, affordability, and are suitable for those who prefer vegan and sustainable beauty options.</p></p>
