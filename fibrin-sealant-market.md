<p><p>Fibrin sealant, also known as fibrin glue, is a biological tissue adhesive composed of two key components: fibrinogen and thrombin. When these two components are mixed together, they form a clotting agent that helps in sealing tissues and promoting wound healing. Fibrin sealants are widely used in various surgical procedures, such as cardiovascular surgery, general surgery, orthopedic surgery, and cosmetic surgery.</p><p>The fibrin sealant market is expected to exhibit substantial growth in the coming years.
