<p><strong>The High Volume Coffee Grinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Burr</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High volume coffee grinders are divided into two main market types: roller grinders and burr grinders. Roller grinders use a pair of rotating discs with ridges to crush the coffee beans, resulting in a more consistent grind size. On the other hand, burr grinders use two metal or ceramic burrs to crush the beans between them, offering greater precision and control over grind size. Both types are commonly utilized in the commercial coffee industry due to their ability to handle large quantities of coffee efficiently.</p></p>
