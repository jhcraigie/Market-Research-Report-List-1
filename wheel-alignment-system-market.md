A 3D Wheel Aligner uses cameras and sensors to accurately measure and analyze wheel angles and alignments. CCD Wheel Aligners use charged coupled device sensors to capture wheel positions and movements. Laser Wheel Aligners utilize laser beams to measure wheel angles. These systems play a crucial role in the automobile industry by ensuring proper wheel alignment, thus improving vehicle performance, safety, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
