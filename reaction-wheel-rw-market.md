<p><p>A Reaction Wheel (RW) is a type of momentum exchange device used in spacecraft to control their attitude or orientation in space. It consists of a rapidly spinning wheel whose angular momentum can be adjusted by applying torque. By changing the wheel's momentum, the spacecraft can counteract external torques and maintain a stable position.</p><p>The RW market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for satellites and spacecraft across various applications such as communication, weather monitoring, and earth observation has been a major driver for the market. Additionally, the growing investments in space exploration activities by both government and private entities have further fueled the demand for RW systems.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of lightweight and efficient RW systems, which offer enhanced operational capabilities.
