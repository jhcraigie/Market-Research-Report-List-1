<p><p>Polyvinylidene difluoride (PVDF) membrane market is segmented into two types - hydrophobic PVDF membrane and hydrophilic PVDF membrane. Hydrophobic PVDF membrane repels water and is commonly used in applications where water intrusion is a concern, such as filtration of organic solvents. On the other hand, hydrophilic PVDF membrane attracts water and is often used in applications where increased wettability and improved flow rates are required, such as in protein binding and cell culture.</p></p>
