<p><p>The metal carports market is divided into two main types: Aluminium and Steel. On the other hand, steel carports are known for their strength and durability. They provide excellent protection against harsh weather conditions and are often preferred in regions with strong winds or heavy snowfall. Both aluminium and steel carports offer a reliable and cost-effective solution for protecting vehicles from the elements.</p></p>
