<p><p>The Medical Sealing Machine market consists of two main types: Continuous Non-print Sealer and Continuous Print Sealer. Continuous Non-print Sealer refers to a sealing machine that can continuously seal medical packages, such as pouches or bags, without printing any information on them. On the other hand, Continuous Print Sealer refers to a machine that can carry out continuous sealing while simultaneously printing essential information, such as batch numbers or expiration dates, on the medical packages. These two types cater to different requirements and preferences within the medical packaging industry.</p></p>
