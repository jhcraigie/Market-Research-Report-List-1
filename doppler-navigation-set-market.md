<p><p>The Doppler Navigation Set market can be categorized into two main types: Continuous Wave and Pulse Wave. In Continuous Wave systems, a continuous radio frequency signal is transmitted and received, allowing for continuous measurement of the target's velocity. On the other hand, Pulse Wave systems use short pulses of radio frequency signals to measure the target's range and velocity. Both types find applications in various industries, such as aviation, marine, and defense, enabling accurate navigation and tracking capabilities in different scenarios.</p></p>
