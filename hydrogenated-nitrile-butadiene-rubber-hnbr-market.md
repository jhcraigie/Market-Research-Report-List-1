<p><p>Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (HNBR) is categorized into three types based on their Acrylonitrile content: Low ACN, Medium ACN, and High ACN. Low ACN HNBR offers excellent low-temperature flexibility and resistance to acids, while High ACN HNBR provides superior heat and oil resistance. Medium ACN HNBR falls in between these two types, offering a balance of properties. Each type serves specific applications depending on the required performance characteristics, making them suitable for a wide range of industries.</p></p>
