This enables data transmission without the constraints of physical cables, offering flexibility and mobility. On the other hand, in the wired data acquisition market, analyzers are connected to industrial systems using cables for data collection. This option provides a secure and reliable data transmission method but lacks the mobility and flexibility of wireless technology.</p></p>
