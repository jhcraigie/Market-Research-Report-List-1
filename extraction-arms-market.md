<p><p>Extraction arms, also known as fume extraction arms or exhaust arms, are specialized mechanical devices used to capture and remove hazardous gases, fumes, dust, and particles from industrial environments such as manufacturing plants, laboratories, and welding shops. These arms are typically made of durable, flexible hoses and are equipped with hoods or nozzles that can be directed towards the source of the pollutants.</p><p>The extraction arms market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to increasing awareness about workplace safety and the need to comply with strict occupational health and safety regulations. The arms help in preventing occupational diseases and maintaining a clean and healthy work environment.</p><p>One of the key factors driving the market growth is the growing industrialization and increase in manufacturing activities across various industries worldwide. The demand for extraction arms is particularly high in sectors such as chemical manufacturing, metal fabrication, automotive, pharmaceuticals, and healthcare.</p><p>Another trend observed in the extraction arms market is the adoption of advanced technologies and innovative design features to enhance the efficiency and flexibility of these devices. Manufacturers are focusing on developing arms with better extraction capacities, increased capture efficiency, and improved maneuverability.</p><p>Moreover, the introduction of integrated extraction systems that combine extraction arms with industrial ventilation systems has further propelled market growth. These systems offer improved air quality control and energy efficiency.</p><p>The forecasted growth rate of the extraction arms market is estimated to be around 11.5% during the forecast period.
