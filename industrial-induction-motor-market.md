<p><ul><li>Squirrel-Cage</li><li>Slip-Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Induction Motor market comprises two main types: Squirrel-Cage and Slip-Ring motors. Squirrel-Cage motors are the most common and widely used. They have a simple and robust design with a cage-like rotor, allowing them to work efficiently across various industrial applications. Slip-Ring motors, on the other hand, have an external rotor circuit connected through slip-rings, enabling control over their speed and torque. These motors are typically used in applications that require high starting torque or variable speed control, such as hoists and cranes.</p></p>
