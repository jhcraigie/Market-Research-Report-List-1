<p><p>A reflux valve, also known as a check valve or non-return valve, is a device used in plumbing systems to prevent the backflow of fluids. With the increasing emphasis on water conservation and efficient plumbing systems, the demand for reflux valves is expected to rise. Manufacturers are focusing on developing reflux valves with improved durability, efficiency, and ease of installation.
