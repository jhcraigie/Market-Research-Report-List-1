<p><p>The electrical sub panel market can be categorized into two types: single phase and three phase sub panels. A single phase sub panel is used in residential settings and smaller commercial spaces, where the electrical supply is divided into two separate phases. On the other hand, a three phase sub panel is utilized in larger commercial or industrial settings, where the electrical supply is divided into three separate phases. These sub panels allow for the distribution of electrical power to various circuits and equipment safely and efficiently.</p></p>
