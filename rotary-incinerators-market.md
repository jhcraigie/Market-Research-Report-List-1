<p><strong>The Rotary Incinerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Counter Current Rotary Incinerators</li><li>Co-Current Rotary Incinerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary incinerators are widely used for waste disposal and management. Counter current rotary incinerators involve the combustion gases flowing in the opposite direction to the waste feed, allowing for better heat transfer and combustion efficiency. Co-current rotary incinerators, on the other hand, involve the waste and combustion gases flowing in the same direction, enabling a higher rate of waste throughput and better mixing. Both types offer different advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements and waste characteristics.</p></p>
