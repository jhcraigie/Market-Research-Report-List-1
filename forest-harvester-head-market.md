<p><p>A forest harvester head, also known as a cutting head or cutting unit, is an attachment used in forestry machinery to cut and harvest trees. It is commonly used in logging operations to fell trees and strip them of their branches before they are transported for processing. These harvester heads typically consist of a cutting saw, delimbing knives, and feeding rollers to efficiently process trees of different sizes.</p><p>The current outlook for the forest harvester head market is positive. The increasing demand for timber and wood products, coupled with advancements in technology, is driving market growth.
