<p><p>The medical vacuum regulator market consists of two types: analog vacuum regulator and digital vacuum regulator. Analog vacuum regulators are traditional devices that use a dial or knob to manually adjust the vacuum pressure. They are simple and cost-effective but may not provide precise control. On the other hand, digital vacuum regulators utilize advanced technology to offer accurate and automated control of vacuum pressure. They have digital displays and allow for precise adjustments, making them more convenient but also more expensive than analog regulators.</p></p>
