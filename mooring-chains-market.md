<p><strong>The Mooring Chains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Link Chain</li><li>Mid Link Chain</li><li>Long Link Chain</li><li>Stud Link Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mooring chains are categorized into four main types based on their link design: short link chain, mid link chain, long link chain, and stud link chain. Short link chains have compact links that provide high strength and flexibility, making them ideal for use in small spaces. Mid link chains offer a balance between strength and flexibility, suitable for various mooring applications. Long link chains have longer links for increased strength and durability. Stud link chains are equipped with studs on each link, providing extra security and stability in demanding marine environments.</p></p>
