<p><p>The Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor Decontamination System market includes two types: Vaporized Type and Fogging Type. In the Vaporized Type, hydrogen peroxide is released in a gaseous state into the environment to decontaminate various surfaces and objects. This method is commonly used in healthcare facilities and pharmaceutical industries. On the other hand, the Fogging Type involves the release of hydrogen peroxide in the form of a fine mist, which is sprayed into the air to reach inaccessible areas for decontamination purposes. This method is commonly employed in large indoor spaces such as laboratories and cleanrooms.</p></p>
