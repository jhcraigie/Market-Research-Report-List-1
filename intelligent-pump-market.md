<p><p>The intelligent pump market comprises two main types of pumps: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps rely on the centrifugal force generated by a rotating impeller to move fluids. They are widely used in industrial applications, including water supply systems and chemical processing plants. On the other hand, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping fluids in enclosed spaces and forcing them out with each stroke. These pumps are commonly employed in industries requiring accurate volume control, such as pharmaceuticals and food processing. Both pump types are crucial in the intelligent pump market, serving various industries with their distinct functionalities.</p></p>
