<p><p>Plastic takeaway containers are available in different types in the market, including PET, PE, PP, and other varieties. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) containers are lightweight, durable, and suitable for cold food products like salads and sandwiches. PE (polyethylene) containers are flexible, affordable, and commonly used for hot food items. PP (polypropylene) containers offer excellent heat resistance and are suitable for microwaving. Other types may include PS (polystyrene) or PLA (polylactic acid) containers, which are commonly used for cold beverages and food. These different plastic takeaway container types cater to various needs and preferences in the food industry.</p></p>
