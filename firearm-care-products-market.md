<p><p>The firearm care products market can be categorized into several types. Lubricants are substances used to reduce friction between firearm parts, ensuring smooth operation and preventing wear and tear. Solvents are chemical solutions that break down and remove carbon, lead, and other residues from the firearm's barrel and other surfaces. Cleaning kits include brushes, rods, and patches designed to physically clean the firearm. Lastly, the "other" market segment includes niche products like rust removers, protectants, and specialized tools for specific types of firearms. These products collectively help maintain and preserve the performance and longevity of firearms.</p></p>
