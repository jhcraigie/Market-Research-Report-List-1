<p><ul><li>Coaxial Circulator</li><li>Drop-in Circulator</li><li>Surface Mount Circulator</li><li>Microstrip Circulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF circulator market consists of different types of circulators, namely coaxial, drop-in, surface mount, and microstrip circulators. Coaxial circulators are widely used and provide high performance levels. Drop-in circulators are compact and offer ease of integration in existing systems. Surface mount circulators are suitable for small form factor applications and are often used in printed circuit boards. Microstrip circulators are integrated into planar circuits and offer excellent isolation and low insertion loss. These different types cater to varying requirements and applications within the RF circulator market.</p></p>
