<p><p>Computer-aided design (CAD) software refers to the use of computer technology to facilitate the creation, modification, analysis, and optimization of design processes. There are two main types of CAD software markets: On-Premise and On-Cloud. On-Premise CAD software is installed locally on the user's computer or server, providing full control over data and operations but requiring dedicated hardware and regular maintenance. On the other hand, On-Cloud CAD software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and simplified maintenance, while data storage and security are entrusted to the service provider.</p></p>
