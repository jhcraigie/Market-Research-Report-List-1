<p><p>An Ultra HD Set Top Box (STB) is a device used to receive television signals and deliver them to a television set in Ultra HD resolution. There are different market types for STBs, including IPTV, Satellite, DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), OTT (Over-The-Top), and Hybrid. IPTV utilizes internet protocol to transmit television content, Satellite uses satellite signals for transmission, DTT relies on terrestrial digital broadcasting, OTT delivers content over the internet, and Hybrid combines different methods, such as internet and satellite, to provide a varied viewing experience to users.</p></p>
