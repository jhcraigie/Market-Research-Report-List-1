<p><p>Study Abroad Application Service market types can be classified into two categories - Entrust the Whole Process and Half-Way Delegation. </p><p>In the Entrust the Whole Process market, service providers handle every aspect of the study abroad application process, including document preparation, application submission, and visa processing. This allows students to completely rely on the service provider for a hassle-free experience.</p><p>On the other hand, the Half-Way Delegation market involves service providers assisting students with certain parts of the application process, such as document preparation or visa processing. Students retain some control and involvement in other areas, such as application submission or communication with universities.</p></p>
