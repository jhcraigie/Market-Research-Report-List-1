<p><p>UPS Power Distribution Cabinet market is divided into two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase. Three Phase systems are designed for larger applications that require higher power capacity, such as data centers, industrial facilities, and large commercial buildings. Both types provide backup power supply and distribution to connected devices during power outages, ensuring continuous operation and protecting sensitive equipment.</p></p>
