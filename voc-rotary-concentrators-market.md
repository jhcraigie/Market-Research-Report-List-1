Zeolite VOC's Rotor utilizes a zeolite material to absorb and concentrate VOCs, while Activated Carbon VOC's Rotor uses activated carbon to adsorb and trap VOCs. Both types offer effective solutions for VOC removal, with Zeolite providing higher adsorption capacity, and Activated Carbon offering versatility for various VOC types and concentrations.</p></p>
