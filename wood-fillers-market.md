<p><p>Wood fillers market can be categorized into two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based wood fillers are composed of organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a solid residue. They are known for their quick drying time and durability. On the other hand, water-based wood fillers use water as a base and have lower VOC levels. They are considered more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. Both types of wood fillers offer effective solutions for repairing and filling imperfections in wood surfaces.</p></p>
