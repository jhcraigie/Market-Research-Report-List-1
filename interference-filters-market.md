<p><p>Interference filters, also known as thin-film filters or dichroic filters, are optical devices used to selectively transmit or block certain wavelengths of light by exploiting the phenomenon of interference. <p><strong>The Interference Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Pass Type</li><li>Low-Pass Type</li><li>Bandpass Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interference filters are optical devices used to selectively transmit or block certain wavelengths of light. High-pass interference filters allow light with higher frequencies to pass through while blocking lower frequencies. Low-pass filters, on the other hand, permit lower frequencies to pass through while blocking higher frequencies. Lastly, bandpass filters allow a specific range of frequencies to pass through while blocking others. These filters find applications in various industries such as telecommunications, imaging, spectroscopy, and lighting, where precise control over the transmission or blocking of light wavelengths is required.</p></p>
