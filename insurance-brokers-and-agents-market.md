<p><p>Insurance brokers and insurance agents are both market types within the insurance industry. Insurance brokers act as intermediaries between clients and insurance companies. They analyze the client's insurance needs, shop around for coverage options, and provide recommendations. Brokers work in the best interest of the client and are typically paid a commission from the insurance company. On the other hand, insurance agents represent specific insurance companies and sell their policies directly to clients. Agents are knowledgeable about the products they sell and work on behalf of the company, earning a commission for each policy they sell.</p></p>
