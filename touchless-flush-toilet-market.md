<p><p>The touchless flush toilet market is divided into two types: radar sensor and infrared sensor. Radar sensor-based toilets use radio waves to detect the presence of a user and initiate the flushing process accordingly. On the other hand, infrared sensor-based toilets use infrared technology to detect human motion, triggering the flush when the user moves away. Both types offer the convenience of a hands-free operation, promoting hygiene and reducing the risk of germ transmission. These technologies play a crucial role in modernizing sanitation systems and increasing user comfort in public and private settings.</p></p>
