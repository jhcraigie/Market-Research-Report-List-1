<p><p>Bearing isolators are devices used to protect bearings and other rotating equipment from contaminants and prevent lubricant leakage. Metallic bearing isolators are made of materials like stainless steel and cast iron, providing durability and resistance to high temperatures and chemicals. Non-metallic bearing isolators, on the other hand, are typically made of synthetic polymers or elastomers, offering corrosion resistance and flexibility. Both types function by creating a labyrinth seal that prevents the ingress of contaminants and the escape of lubricants, ensuring efficient and reliable operation of rotating equipment.</p></p>
