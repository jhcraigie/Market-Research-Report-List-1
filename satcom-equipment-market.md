<p><p>SATCOM equipment refers to various devices used in satellite communication systems. SATCOM modem/router enables data transmission between ground stations and satellites. SATCOM receiver decodes satellite signals for information retrieval. SATCOM transmitter/transponder sends and receives signals to and from satellites. SATCOM transceiver combines the functions of both transmitter and receiver. SATCOM antenna is used to transmit and receive signals with satellites. These equipment types are essential components of satellite communication systems, facilitating data communication in various industries such as broadcasting, military communication, and maritime communication.</p></p>
