<p><p>Organic Light-emitting Diodes (OLEDs) Microdisplay market consists of two main types: Reflective Microdisplay and Transmissive Microdisplay. Reflective Microdisplays use a reflective surface to bounce light back towards the viewer, resulting in high brightness and energy efficiency. On the other hand, Transmissive Microdisplays allow light to pass through, enabling vibrant colors and high contrast ratios. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific application, such as augmented reality devices, head-mounted displays, and automotive displays.</p></p>
