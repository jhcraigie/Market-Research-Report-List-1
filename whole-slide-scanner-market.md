<p><strong>The Whole Slide Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brightfield</li><li>Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Whole Slide Scanner Market Types:</p><p>Brightfield: Brightfield whole slide scanners are designed to capture and digitize images of tissue samples using regular white light. These scanners are ideal for routine pathology applications as they allow for visualizing cellular structures and staining patterns in traditional histopathology preparations.</p><p>Fluorescence: Fluorescence whole slide scanners enable the capture and digitization of fluorescently stained specimens, where specific molecules or structures are labeled with fluorescent dyes. These scanners are used in advanced research settings to study cellular processes, protein localization, and molecular interactions in various biomedical fields such as immunofluorescence, molecular pathology, and cytology.</p></p>
