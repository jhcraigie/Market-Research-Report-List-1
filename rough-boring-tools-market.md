<p><p>The rough boring tools market finds application in various industries, including pre-machining, casting, and forging. In casting, rough boring tools help in creating precise holes and cavities in the casting mold for the molten material to be poured into. Similarly, in forging, these tools are employed to create cavities or remove excess material to shape the forged components accurately.</p></p>
