<p><p>HJT (heterojunction technology) whole line equipment market types refer to the different categories of equipment involved in the production of HJT solar cells. PECVD (plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition) equipment is used for depositing thin films of materials onto the solar cell substrate. PVD (physical vapor deposition) equipment is used for creating thin films by a process of condensation. Screen printing equipment is used for applying the necessary conductive and passivating layers on the solar cell. These equipment types play crucial roles in manufacturing the HJT solar cells efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
