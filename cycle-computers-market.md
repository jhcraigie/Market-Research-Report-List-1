<p><p>The cycle computers market can be divided into three main types: wired computer, wireless computer, and wireless & GPS computer. Wired computers are connected to the bike's wheel and calculate distance and speed based on wheel rotations. Wireless computers use wireless transmission to display information from sensors on the bike, such as speed, cadence, and heart rate, without the need for cables. Wireless & GPS computers combine the features of wireless computers with built-in GPS capabilities to provide navigation and advanced tracking functionalities.</p></p>
