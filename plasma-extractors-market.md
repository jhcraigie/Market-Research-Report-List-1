<p><p>Plasma extractors are devices used to collect plasma from blood samples. There are two main types: manual and automatic plasma extractors. Manual extractors require the operator to manually separate plasma from the blood using gravity or centrifugation methods. On the other hand, automatic extractors utilize advanced technology to automate the plasma extraction process, providing higher efficiency and accuracy. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the healthcare industry, offering options for manual control or automated operation.</p></p>
