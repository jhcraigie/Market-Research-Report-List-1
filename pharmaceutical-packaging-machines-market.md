<p><p>Pharmaceutical Packaging Machines refer to machines used in the pharmaceutical industry to package medicines and related products. Primary Packaging Machines are responsible for the initial packaging of pharmaceutical products, such as filling bottles or blister packs. Secondary Packaging Machines handle the secondary packaging of products, including grouping primary packages into boxes or cartons. Labeling and Serialization Machines apply labels with important product information and unique serial numbers to ensure traceability and prevent counterfeiting. These machines play a crucial role in enhancing the safety, efficiency, and compliance of pharmaceutical packaging processes.</p></p>
