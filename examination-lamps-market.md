<p><p>The examination lamps market is categorized into three types: LED, Halogen, and Fluorescent. LED lamps utilize light-emitting diodes to produce bright and energy-efficient illumination. Halogen lamps use a tungsten filament within a halogen gas envelope to generate intense and focused light. Fluorescent lamps generate light through the excitation of gases and are known for their long lifespan and cost-effectiveness. These different types cater to various customer preferences and requirements, providing a range of options in the examination lamps market.</p></p>
