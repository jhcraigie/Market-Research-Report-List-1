<p><p>Breast cancer screening market consists of various types of screening methods including mammography screening, breast magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) screening, and breast ultrasound screening. Mammography screening involves using low-dose X-rays to detect abnormalities in breast tissue. Breast MRI screening utilizes magnetic waves and contrast agents to produce detailed images of breast tissue. Breast ultrasound screening employs sound waves to create images, particularly useful for evaluating dense breast tissue. These screening methods aid in early detection and diagnosis of breast cancer, enabling timely treatment and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
