<p><p>Conversational AI platforms refer to technologies that enable automated interactions between computers and humans through natural language. The market for these platforms can be divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based conversational AI platforms are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and utilize them over the internet. On the other hand, on-premise conversational AI platforms are installed and managed locally on the user's own infrastructure.
