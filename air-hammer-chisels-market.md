<p><strong>The Air Hammer Chisels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turning Hammer Chisels</li><li>Non Turning Hammer Chisels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air hammer chisels are categorized into two main types: turning hammer chisels and non-turning hammer chisels. </p><p>Turning hammer chisels are designed to rotate or turn inside the air hammer, which helps in creating a rolling effect, enhancing productivity and reducing operator fatigue. These chisels are commonly used in applications that require continuous chiseling or cutting.</p><p>On the other hand, non-turning hammer chisels do not rotate or turn inside the air hammer. They are predominantly used in tasks that involve concrete breaking, rock chiseling, or general demolition work. These chisels provide powerful and direct impact to break tough materials effectively.</p></p>
