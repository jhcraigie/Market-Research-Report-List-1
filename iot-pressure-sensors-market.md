<p><p>The IoT Pressure Sensors market consists of various types of sensors used to measure different types of pressure. Absolute Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, while Gauge Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Vacuum Pressure Sensors measure pressures below atmospheric levels. Differential Pressure Sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points. Sealed Pressure Sensors are designed to operate in harsh environments and are sealed to prevent damage. These sensors are widely used in industries such as automotive, healthcare, and manufacturing to monitor and control pressure-related processes.</p></p>
