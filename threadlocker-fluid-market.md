<p><p>Threadlocker fluids are categorized into three main types based on their strength levels: Low strength level is designed for small fasteners and can be easily removed using hand tools. Medium strength level provides a secure hold for medium-sized fasteners and can be removed with standard hand tools. High strength level offers the most secure bond for large fasteners and requires heat or specialized tools for removal. Each type caters to different needs in various industries, providing options for different strength requirements.</p></p>
