<p><p>The roadway dispenser market comprises two main types: manual and electric dispensers. Manual dispensers are operated by hand and require physical effort to dispense materials such as asphalt or concrete onto road surfaces. They are generally more affordable but can be less efficient and slower compared to electric dispensers. Electric dispensers, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and provide automated and precise material dispensing. They are faster, more accurate, and require less labor, but tend to be more expensive than manual dispensers. Both types cater to different needs and budgets in the roadway construction industry.</p></p>
