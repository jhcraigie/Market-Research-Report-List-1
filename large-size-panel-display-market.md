<p><p>The large size panel display market comprises two main types: AMOLED and LCD. AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) is a display technology that utilizes organic compounds to emit light when an electric current is applied. It offers better contrast, wider viewing angles, and faster response times compared to LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technology. LCD panels work by manipulating light through liquid crystals controlled by electric currents. They are known for their high brightness, energy efficiency, and wide color gamut. Both AMOLED and LCD technologies play a significant role in the large size panel display market.</p></p>
