<p><strong>The Thin-Film Deposition Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVD Equipment</li><li>CVD Equipment</li><li>ALD Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thin-film deposition machine market comprises three main types: PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment, CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment, and ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) equipment. PVD equipment involves the deposition of materials through physical processes such as evaporation or sputtering. CVD equipment utilizes chemical reactions to deposit thin films, while ALD equipment facilitates the deposition of films layer by layer through self-limiting surface reactions. These different types of machines offer various advantages and applications, catering to the diverse needs of industries such as electronics, optics, and medical.</p></p>
