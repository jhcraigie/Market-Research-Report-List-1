<p><strong>The Universal Rail Mounted Temperature Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Universal Rail Mounted Temperature Transmitters market includes three types: 2-Wire, 3-Wire, and 4-Wire. </p><p>In a 2-Wire transmitter, both power and communication signals are carried through the same two wires, simplifying installation and reducing wiring costs. </p><p>A 3-Wire transmitter separates the power and communication signals, utilizing one wire for power supply and two wires for communication, ensuring better accuracy.</p><p>A 4-Wire transmitter provides separate wires for both power supply and communication signals, resulting in the highest level of accuracy and flexibility in temperature measurement.</p></p>
