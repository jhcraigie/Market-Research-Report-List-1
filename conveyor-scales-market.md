<p><p>Conveyor scales are used to accurately measure the weight of materials on a moving conveyor belt. There are different types of conveyor scales available in the market. Single-idler conveyor scales consist of a single weighing idler that supports and measures the weight of the material. Two-idler conveyor scales have two weighing idlers for improved stability and accuracy. Multi-idler conveyor scales have multiple weighing idlers, evenly distributed along the belt, allowing for even greater accuracy and stability. Each type of conveyor scale offers different features and benefits, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
