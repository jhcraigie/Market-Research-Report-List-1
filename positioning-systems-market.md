<p><p>Positioning systems are technologies that are used to determine the location, position, and orientation of objects or individuals in real-time. The market is driven by the growing adoption of Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS) such as GPS, GLONASS, and Galileo, which provide precise positioning information. Additionally, the rising use of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and autonomous vehicles is fueling the demand for advanced positioning systems.</p><p>Moreover, the emergence of technologies like Real-Time Kinematic (RTK), Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) augmentation systems, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) integrated positioning systems is boosting market growth.
