<p><p>Cable and wire markers refer to specialized products used to label and identify cables and wires in various industries. Two common types are write-on cable and wire markers and print-on cable and wire markers. Write-on markers are manually labeled using a marker or pen, providing flexibility for on-site adjustments. Print-on markers, on the other hand, enable users to print labels using thermal transfer printers or inkjet systems, allowing for more professional and customizable labeling. Both types play a crucial role in organizing and managing wire and cable systems efficiently.</p></p>
