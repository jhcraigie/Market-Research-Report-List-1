<p><p>Pipettes are essential tools used in laboratories and medical settings for accurate dispensing and transferring of liquids. There are several types of pipettes available in the market. Capillary pipettes have a very fine capillary tube which allows for precise measurements of small volumes. Pasteur pipettes are widely used for transferring larger volumes of liquids. Serological pipettes are designed for measuring and transferring medium volumes in a controlled manner. Volumetric pipettes are highly accurate and used for delivering a specific volume of liquid. Piston pipettes are commonly used for repetitive dispensing of liquids in a controlled manner.</p></p>
