<p><p>The thermoelectric semiconductor market is categorized into two types: single stage module and multistage module. In single stage modules, only one set of thermoelectric materials is used, while in multistage modules, multiple sets of thermoelectric materials are arranged in series. Single stage modules are suitable for applications requiring low temperature differences, while multistage modules are employed in high temperature difference scenarios. Both types find applications in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, consumer electronics, aerospace, and industrial sectors, where they provide energy-efficient cooling, heating, and power generation solutions.</p></p>
