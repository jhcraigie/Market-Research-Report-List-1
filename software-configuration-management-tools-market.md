<p><p>Software Configuration Management (SCM) tools are used to manage and control software development processes. The market for these tools can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based SCM tools are hosted on external servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On-premise SCM tools, on the other hand, are installed and operated within the user's own infrastructure, providing greater control over security and customization. Both types have their advantages and choosing between them depends on factors like budget, security requirements, and organizational preferences.</p></p>
