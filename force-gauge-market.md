<p><p>The force gauge market consists of two main types: digital force gauges and mechanical force gauges. Digital force gauges utilize electronic sensors and display screens to measure and display force readings. They are known for their accuracy, ease of use, and ability to store and analyze data. Mechanical force gauges, on the other hand, rely on mechanical components such as springs and levers to measure force. They are simpler in design and require manual reading of force values. Both types cater to different customer preferences and applications.</p></p>
