<p><strong>The Heliport Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High intensity lights (HIL)</li><li>Medium intensity lights (MIL)</li><li>Low intensity lights (LIL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heliport Lighting Market is divided into three types based on the intensity of lights used for aircraft navigation. High intensity lights (HIL) are the brightest and most powerful lights, suitable for large helipads or airports. Medium intensity lights (MIL) are slightly less bright and are commonly used for medium-sized helipads or military bases. Low intensity lights (LIL) are the least powerful lights used for small helipads or private landing areas. These different types cater to specific needs and requirements of heliports, ensuring safe and efficient operations.</p></p>
