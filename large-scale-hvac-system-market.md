<p><p>Large-scale HVAC systems can be categorized into two main types: decentralized HVAC systems and centralized HVAC systems. Decentralized HVAC systems consist of multiple individual units located within separate areas, providing independent control and management options for each space. Centralized HVAC systems, on the other hand, involve a single unit or system that serves the entire building, controlling the temperature and air quality throughout. Each type offers its own advantages and considerations, depending on the specific requirements and demands of the building or facility.</p></p>
