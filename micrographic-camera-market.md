<p><strong>The Micrographic Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Micrograph</li><li>Electron Micrograph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micrographic camera market can be divided into two types: optical micrograph and electron micrograph. </p><p>An optical micrograph camera captures images using visible light. It is commonly used in fields like biology and material sciences, providing high-resolution images with detailed information.</p><p>On the other hand, an electron micrograph camera uses a beam of accelerated electrons to obtain images. It can provide extremely high magnification and resolution, enabling researchers in fields such as nanotechnology and semiconductor research to examine samples at the atomic level.</p></p>
