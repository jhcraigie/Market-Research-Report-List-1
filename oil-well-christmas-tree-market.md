HPHT Christmas Trees are designed to withstand extreme pressures and temperatures found in deep wells. Horizontal Christmas Trees are utilized in wells where the formation is reached horizontally, allowing for increased oil recovery. Vertical Christmas Trees are used in traditional vertical wells.
