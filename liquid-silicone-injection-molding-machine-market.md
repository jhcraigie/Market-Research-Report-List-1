<p><p>The liquid silicone injection molding machine market consists of two types: horizontal injection and vertical injection. In horizontal injection machines, the injection unit is placed horizontally, allowing for easy access and operation. This type is suitable for larger molds and high-volume production. On the other hand, vertical injection machines have a vertical injection unit, making them more compact and suitable for smaller molds and low-volume production. Both types offer precise control and efficiency in molding liquid silicone products.</p></p>
