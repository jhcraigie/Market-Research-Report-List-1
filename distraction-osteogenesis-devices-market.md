<p><ul><li>Internal Distractors</li><li>External Distractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distraction Osteogenesis Devices market is categorized into two types: Internal Distractors and External Distractors. Internal distractors are implantable devices that are placed directly into the bone to facilitate bone growth and movement. They are typically used in complex cases or for precise control over the distraction process. On the other hand, external distractors are devices that are attached externally to the bone using pins or screws. They are more commonly used in simple cases and allow for easy adjustment and monitoring of the distraction process.</p></p>
