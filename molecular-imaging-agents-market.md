<p><p>The molecular imaging agents market can be categorized into various types based on the composition of the agents. Small molecules are organic compounds that can easily penetrate cells, whereas peptides are short amino acid chains that can specifically bind to target tissues. Engineered protein fragments are modified proteins designed to interact with specific targets. Aptamers are short single-stranded nucleic acids that can bind to specific molecules. Nanoparticles are tiny particles that can be functionalized to carry imaging agents. Lastly, "Others" encompass miscellaneous types of imaging agents used for molecular imaging purposes.</p></p>
