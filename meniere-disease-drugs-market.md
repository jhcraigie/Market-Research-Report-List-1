<p><p>The Meniere Disease Drugs market can be categorized into several types. Vestibular inhibitors aim to suppress the vestibular system and reduce symptoms like dizziness and vertigo. Anticholinergics block the action of a neurotransmitter, reducing excessive signals to the brain. Vasodilators are used to expand blood vessels and increase blood flow to the inner ear. Diuretic dehydration medicine helps reduce fluid retention, alleviating pressure in the inner ear. Glucocorticoids have anti-inflammatory effects, reducing inflammation in the ear. Lastly, other drugs targeting Meniere Disease may include migraine medications and sedatives.</p></p>
