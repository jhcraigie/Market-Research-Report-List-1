<p><p>Fracking Fluids and Chemicals have different market types based on their composition. Water Based Fluids (WBF) are primarily composed of water, with additives like gelling agents. Oil Based Fluids (OBF) contain petroleum-based components, improving lubrication and reducing friction. Synthetic Based Fluids (SBF) are chemically engineered, offering advantages of both water and oil-based fluids. Foam Based Fluids (FBF) contain gases mixed in with water or oil, creating a foam-like consistency. Each market type has its own benefits and applications in hydraulic fracturing operations.</p></p>
