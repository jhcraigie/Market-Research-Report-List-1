These are commonly used in supermarkets and convenience stores, allowing easy access to products while maintaining temperature control. On the other hand, sliding door type merchandisers have doors that slide open horizontally, providing a sleek and space-saving design. These are often found in smaller retail establishments and cafes where space might be limited.</p></p>
