<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Portable LED projectors come in two main technology types - DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors use microscopic mirrors to reflect light, producing high-quality images with great contrast and color accuracy. On the other hand, LCoS projectors use liquid crystals sandwiched between a reflective surface and a glass substrate to project images, offering high-resolution output and smooth visuals. Both technologies provide portable LED projectors with innovative display capabilities, catering to different market preferences and requirements.</p></p>
