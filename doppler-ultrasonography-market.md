<p><p>Doppler Ultrasonography is a medical imaging technique that assesses blood flow and other abnormalities in the body. The Color Doppler Ultrasound market focuses on displaying blood flow direction and velocity using color-coded images. Power Doppler Ultrasound market emphasizes the detection of blood flow signals, particularly in organs with low blood flow. Spectral Doppler Ultrasound market measures blood flow velocity using a graph display. Duplex Doppler Ultrasound combines color imaging and spectral Doppler to provide comprehensive information. Continuous Wave Doppler Ultrasound market uses ultrasound waves to measure blood flow velocity at specific points in the body.</p></p>
