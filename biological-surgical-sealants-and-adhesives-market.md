<p><p>Biological surgical sealants and adhesives are types of medical products used to seal or bind tissues during surgical procedures. Fibrin-based sealants are made from blood-derived proteins and are effective in promoting clot formation. Collagen-based sealants, derived from bovine or porcine sources, provide strength and promote tissue healing. Albumin-based sealants use human albumin protein and are effective in sealing small vessels and reducing tissue inflammation. These types of sealants and adhesives are commonly used in various surgical applications to improve patient outcomes and reduce complications.</p></p>
