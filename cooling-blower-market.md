<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The cooling blower market can be categorized into two types: brushed and brushless. Brushed cooling blowers are equipped with a brush and commutator system, where the brush comes into contact with the commutator to generate a continuous motion. On the other hand, brushless cooling blowers use electronic commutation through sensors and magnets to control the movement of the rotor. Brushless blowers are generally more efficient, produce less noise, have a longer lifespan, and require less maintenance compared to brushed blowers.</p></p>
