<p><ul><li>Loading/Unloading</li><li>Screening</li><li>Transport</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Catalyst handling services are divided into three main market types: loading/unloading, screening, and transport. Loading/unloading services involve the safe and efficient transfer of catalyst materials into and out of reactors and vessels. Screening services involve the separation of catalyst materials based on size or composition. Transport services involve the movement of catalyst materials between different locations, such as from a storage facility to a processing plant. Each of these market types plays a crucial role in ensuring the proper handling and utilization of catalyst materials in industrial processes.</p></p>
