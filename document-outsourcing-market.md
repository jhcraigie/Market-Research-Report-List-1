<p><p>Document outsourcing is a practice where businesses delegate document-related tasks to external service providers. Three common types of document outsourcing markets include onsite contracted services, statement printing services, and Document Process Outsourcing (DPO) services. Onsite contracted services involve hiring external professionals to work directly at the company's premises. Statement printing services focus on printing and mailing statements, such as invoices or bills, on behalf of businesses. DPO services encompass the entire document lifecycle, including data capture, processing, storage, and retrieval, providing comprehensive solutions for businesses seeking to outsource their document management needs.</p></p>
