<p><p>An electrolysis oxygen concentrator is a medical device that extracts oxygen from the surrounding air through a process called electrolysis. The primary factor driving this growth is the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma. This rise in respiratory diseases has led to a surge in demand for oxygen therapy devices, including electrolysis oxygen concentrators.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have resulted in the development of portable and lightweight electrolysis oxygen concentrators, which are more convenient for patients to use.
