<p><p>The electric pole saw market can be categorized into two types: corded and cordless. Corded pole saws are powered by electricity and require a constant connection to a power source. They typically offer a higher cutting power but limited mobility due to the cord. On the other hand, cordless pole saws are powered by rechargeable batteries, providing greater freedom of movement. They are ideal for areas without easy access to electricity but may have slightly lower cutting power compared to corded models.</p></p>
