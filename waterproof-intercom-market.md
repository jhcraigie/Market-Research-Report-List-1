<p><p>The waterproof intercom market consists of two main types: wall-mounted intercoms and flush-mount intercoms. Wall-mounted intercoms are devices installed directly on the wall surface, allowing easy access and visibility. They are commonly used in homes, offices, and public spaces. On the other hand, flush-mount intercoms are designed to be mounted inside the wall, giving a more streamlined and aesthetically pleasing appearance. These intercoms are often utilized in high-end residential or commercial buildings where a seamless integration with the wall is desired. Both types of intercoms are designed to withstand water exposure and provide effective communication in various settings.</p></p>
