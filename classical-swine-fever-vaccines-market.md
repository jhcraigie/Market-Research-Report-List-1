<p><p>Classical Swine Fever Vaccines can be categorized into two types: Tissue Culture Origin and Cell Line Origin. Tissue Culture Origin vaccines are manufactured using cells derived from swine tissue, which are then cultured and inactivated to create the vaccine. On the other hand, Cell Line Origin vaccines are produced using continuous cell lines, such as porcine kidney or testicular cells, which are infected and inactivated to develop the vaccine.
