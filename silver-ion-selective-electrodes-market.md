<p><p>Silver ion selective electrodes are used in analytical chemistry to measure the concentration of silver ions in a sample. There are two types of silver ion selective electrodes available in the market: crystal membrane and PVC membrane. Crystal membrane electrodes use a solid crystal membrane made of a silver salt, which selectively binds with silver ions. PVC membrane electrodes, on the other hand, use a PVC-based membrane containing a silver complexing agent. Both types of electrodes provide reliable and accurate measurements of silver ion concentrations in various applications.</p></p>
