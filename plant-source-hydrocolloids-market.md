Hemicellulose is another type of plant-derived hydrocolloid that is gaining popularity for its emulsifying and gelling properties. Pectin is a natural hydrocolloid found in fruits and is commonly used in jams and jellies. Exudate gums are plant secretions that have adhesive properties, while other markets include various plant-based hydrocolloids with unique functionalities.</p></p>
