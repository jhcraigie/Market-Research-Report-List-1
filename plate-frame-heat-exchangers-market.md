<p><p>The Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers market consists of three types: Brazed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers, Gasketed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers, and Welded Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers. </p><p>Brazed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers are compact and efficient, with plates brazed together for snug fit. They require no gaskets and are ideal for high pressure and temperature applications.</p><p>Gasketed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers feature plates held together by gaskets, allowing for easy disassembly and cleaning. They are versatile and can handle a wide range of applications.</p><p>Welded Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers have plates welded together, providing excellent resistance to high pressures and temperatures. They are highly durable and suitable for demanding environments.</p></p>
