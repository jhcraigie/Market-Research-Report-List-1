They are commonly used for crushing large chunks of materials. On the other hand, double axis chain mills have two rotors, each with evenly spaced chains, which provide a higher level of crushing efficiency and are suitable for finer crushing operations. Both types of chain mills are employed in applications such as mining, agriculture, and chemical industries.</p></p>
