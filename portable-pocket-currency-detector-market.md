<p><p>Portable pocket currency detectors come in various types, including fluorescence detection and magnetic detection. Fluorescence detection is based on the use of ultraviolet light to illuminate security features on banknotes that react by emitting a fluorescent glow. Magnetic detection, on the other hand, relies on the presence of magnetic inks used in the printing of currency. Both types of currency detectors are popular in the market due to their portability, ease of use, and efficiency in detecting counterfeit money.</p></p>
