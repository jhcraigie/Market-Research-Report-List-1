<p><strong>The Restaurant Catering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The restaurant catering systems market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based catering systems operate entirely online, allowing restaurants to access the system and its features via the internet. On the other hand, on-premises catering systems are installed and hosted locally in the restaurant's physical infrastructure. While cloud-based systems offer flexibility, remote accessibility, and automatic updates, on-premises systems provide greater control over data security and customization options. Choosing between the two depends on a restaurant's particular needs and preferences.</p></p>
