<p><p>The Wheels & Axles market for railways can be categorized into two types: rolled wheels & axles, and forged wheels & axles. Rolled wheels & axles are manufactured by elongating and shaping metal sheets through rolling processes. This method provides cost-effective solutions and is commonly used for lighter loads and less demanding rail applications. On the other hand, forged wheels & axles are made by heating and shaping solid metal pieces through forging techniques. This process results in stronger and more durable components, making them suitable for heavy-duty and high-performance railway operations.</p></p>
