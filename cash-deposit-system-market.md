<p><p>The cash deposit system market consists of two main types: standing type and embedded type. The standing type refers to standalone cash deposit machines or kiosks that are placed in public areas such as banks or shopping malls, allowing users to deposit cash directly into their accounts without the need for human assistance. On the other hand, the embedded type refers to cash deposit systems that are integrated into other devices such as ATMs or self-service machines, providing users with a convenient way to deposit cash while performing other transactions. Both types aim to enhance efficiency and convenience in cash deposit operations.</p></p>
