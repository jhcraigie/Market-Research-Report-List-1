<p><p>Graphite bearings are used in various industries due to their self-lubricating and high-temperature resistant properties. Radial bearings are designed to support radial loads perpendicular to the shaft, while axial bearings support axial loads parallel to the shaft. Radial bearings are commonly used in motors, fans, and pumps, while axial bearings are used in applications like gearboxes and thrust reversers. Both types of bearings play a crucial role in enabling smooth and efficient rotational movement in different mechanical systems, enhancing their overall performance and durability.</p></p>
