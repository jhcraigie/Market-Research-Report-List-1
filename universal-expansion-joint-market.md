<p><p>The Universal Expansion Joint Market includes two main types: Pipe End Type and Flange Type. In the Pipe End Type, the joint is connected directly to the pipes, allowing for movement in all directions. This type is commonly used when there is limited space. On the other hand, the Flange Type is connected to the pipes through flanges, providing flexibility and ease of installation. This type allows for angular movement and is often preferred when there is sufficient space.</p></p>
