<p><p>The Natural Gas Internal Combustion Engine (NGICE) market comprises two main types: Spark Ignition (SI) and Compression Ignition (CI) engines. SI engines use a spark plug to ignite the fuel-air mixture, resulting in a controlled combustion process. These engines are commonly used in light-duty vehicles and small-scale power generation. On the other hand, CI engines rely on high compression to ignite the fuel-air mixture, enhancing efficiency and power. They are mostly found in heavy-duty vehicles, such as trucks, buses, and some power generation applications. Both types play a significant role in the expanding NGICE market.</p></p>
