<p><p>The nasal oxygen cannula market consists of two main types: low-flow nasal oxygen cannula and high-flow nasal oxygen cannula. Low-flow cannula delivers oxygen at a rate of 1-6 liters per minute and is commonly used for patients with mild to moderate respiratory conditions. On the other hand, high-flow cannula provides a higher oxygen flow rate of up to 60 liters per minute, making it suitable for patients with severe respiratory conditions or those in need of more oxygen support. Both types of cannula are essential medical devices for delivering oxygen therapy to patients.</p></p>
