<p><p>The biohazard suit market consists of two major types: disposable suits and reusable suits. Disposable suits are designed to be used once and then discarded. They provide maximum protection against hazardous materials but are not cost-effective for long-term use. Reusable suits, on the other hand, can be re-worn multiple times after washing and decontamination. While they offer similar protection as disposable suits, they are favored for their sustainability and lower overall cost in scenarios where regular use is required. Both types are crucial in ensuring the safety of individuals working in high-risk environments.</p></p>
