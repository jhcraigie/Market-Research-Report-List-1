<p><p>The 300mm wafer cases market comprises two primary types: Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP). FOSB is a container used for shipping and storing wafers, providing protection during transportation. On the other hand, FOUP is a specialized case designed for storing wafers within semiconductor manufacturing facilities, providing a controlled environment to prevent contamination. Both FOSB and FOUP play crucial roles in safeguarding the delicate wafers and ensuring their integrity and quality during shipping and storage processes.</p></p>
