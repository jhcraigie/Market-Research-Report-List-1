<p><p>A Medical Alert System, also known as a Personal Emergency Response System (PERS), is a device that enables individuals to call for help during emergencies such as accidents, falls, or medical emergencies. It typically consists of a wearable device, often a pendant or wristband, and a base station connected to a monitoring center. In case of an emergency, the user can press a button on the wearable device, which sends a signal to the base station, connecting them to a trained operator who can dispatch emergency services or contact a designated caregiver.</p><p>The Medical Alert System & Personal Emergency Response System Market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. The increasing geriatric population, rising prevalence of chronic diseases, and the growing importance of independent living among the elderly are driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the integration of GPS and fall detection features, are further fueling the demand for medical alert systems.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing a shift towards mobile-based systems, enabling users to access emergency help from anywhere using their smartphones. This trend is being driven by the widespread adoption of smartphones and the convenience they offer.
