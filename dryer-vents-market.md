<p><p>A dryer vent is a crucial component of a laundry system that helps remove moisture and lint from the dryer and expels it outdoors. It typically consists of a duct or pipe that connects the dryer to an external vent opening, ensuring proper ventilation and preventing potential hazards such as fires and indoor air pollution.</p><p>The global dryer vents market has been experiencing steady growth and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for energy-efficient and technologically advanced dryers, along with the rise in environmental concerns, is driving the market growth. Consumers are becoming more aware of the importance of proper dryer venting systems, leading to an increased adoption of dryer vents to ensure safety and energy efficiency.</p><p>One of the key trends in the dryer vents market is the growing preference for flexible and rigid ducts. Flexible ducts are gaining popularity due to their flexibility, ease of installation, and suitability for complex and tight spaces. Rigid ducts, on the other hand, offer better airflow and durability. Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative and high-quality duct materials to cater to the evolving consumer demand.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing an increasing demand for dryer vents with advanced features such as pest barriers and improved efficiency. These features not only enhance the overall performance of the dryer vent system but also provide added convenience to consumers.</p><p>Overall, the dryer vents market is poised for significant growth in the coming years as consumers prioritize safety, energy efficiency, and enhanced functionality in their laundry systems.</p></p>
